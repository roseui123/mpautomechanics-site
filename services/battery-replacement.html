Professional Car Battery Services

MP Auto Mechanics provides professional car battery replacement services in Melbourne for cars, SUVs, commercial vehicles and 4WDs.


Weak or damaged batteries can cause starting problems, electrical faults and unexpected breakdowns. We perform battery testing and diagnostics before replacement to ensure the correct solution for your vehicle.

Signs Your Car Battery May Need Replacement

  • Vehicle struggles to start
  • Dashboard battery warning light appears
  • Headlights become dim or flicker
  • Electrical accessories stop working properly
  • Clicking sound during engine start
  • Battery older than 3 to 5 years
  • Corrosion around battery terminals
