Professional Alternator Diagnostics & Repairs

MP Auto Mechanics provides professional alternator repair and charging system diagnostics for cars, SUVs, commercial vehicles and 4WDs across Melbourne.


Faulty alternators can cause battery drain, warning lights, dim headlights, electrical failures and vehicle starting issues. Early diagnosis helps prevent unexpected breakdowns.

Common Signs of Alternator Problems

  • Battery warning light on dashboard
  • Vehicle battery repeatedly going flat
  • Dim or flickering headlights
  • Difficulty starting the engine
  • Electrical accessories not working properly
  • Burning smell or unusual noises

Charging System Inspection

We inspect alternators, charging voltage, battery condition, starter systems and related electrical wiring to accurately locate the fault.


Professional testing equipment helps identify hidden charging system issues quickly and efficiently.
