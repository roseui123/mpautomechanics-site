Professional Alternator Repairs

MP Auto Mechanics provides reliable alternator repair and charging system diagnostics for vehicles across Melbourne. The alternator is responsible for charging your battery and powering your vehicle’s electrical systems while the engine is running. A faulty alternator can quickly lead to battery drain, electrical failures and vehicle breakdowns.


Our experienced auto electricians diagnose alternator problems accurately and provide dependable repair or replacement solutions for most vehicle makes and models. We use professional testing equipment to inspect charging voltage, electrical output and related components.

Common Signs of Alternator Problems

  • Battery warning light on dashboard
  • Vehicle battery repeatedly goes flat
  • Dim headlights or flickering lights
  • Electrical accessories stop working properly
  • Strange whining or grinding noises
  • Burning smell from engine area
  • Difficulty starting the vehicle

Charging System Diagnostics

Not all charging problems are caused by the alternator itself. Faulty wiring, loose connections, damaged belts or weak batteries can also affect charging performance. Our technicians perform complete charging system diagnostics to identify the exact issue before repairs begin.


We test battery voltage, alternator output, belt condition and electrical connections to ensure the charging system operates reliably under all driving conditions.

Preventing Alternator Failure

Regular vehicle servicing and electrical inspections help reduce the risk of alternator failure. Weak batteries, worn drive belts and poor electrical connections can place additional stress on the charging system over time.


Early diagnosis of charging issues can prevent expensive repairs and unexpected roadside breakdowns. If you notice warning lights or electrical performance problems, professional inspection is recommended as soon as possible.
