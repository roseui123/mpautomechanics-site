Advanced Electrical Diagnostics

Modern vehicles use complex lighting control systems and electronic modules. We use professional diagnostic tools to accurately locate faults within the lighting system instead of simply replacing parts unnecessarily.

Our diagnostics help identify damaged wiring, poor grounding connections, faulty relays, switch failures and battery-related lighting issues.
