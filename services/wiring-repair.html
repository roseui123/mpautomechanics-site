Professional Auto Electrical Wiring Repairs

Vehicle wiring systems are responsible for powering important electrical components throughout your car. Damaged, loose or corroded wiring can cause starting problems, battery drain, lighting faults and unexpected electrical failures.


MP Auto Mechanics provides reliable wiring diagnostics and repair services across Melbourne using professional testing equipment and years of hands-on auto electrical experience.

Common Wiring Problems We Repair

  • Damaged or broken wiring
  • Electrical short circuits
  • Blown fuse problems
  • Loose electrical connections
  • Battery drain caused by wiring faults
  • Lighting and headlight wiring issues
  • Trailer wiring repairs
  • Corroded terminals and connectors
  • Engine bay wiring damage
  • Dashboard electrical faults

Accurate Fault Finding

Electrical wiring problems can sometimes be difficult to detect because faults may occur intermittently or affect multiple vehicle systems at the same time.


We carefully inspect wiring circuits, connectors, relays and electrical components to locate the exact cause of the problem before carrying out repairs.
