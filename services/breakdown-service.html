Emergency Roadside Auto Electrical Assistance

Vehicle breakdowns can happen unexpectedly and electrical faults are one of the most common causes. MP Auto Mechanics provides fast and reliable mobile breakdown auto electrical services across Melbourne for cars, vans, and light commercial vehicles.

We diagnose starting problems, battery failures, alternator faults, blown fuses, wiring issues, charging system problems and other electrical failures that prevent your vehicle from operating correctly.

Common Breakdown Problems We Fix

  • Flat or dead car batteries
  • Alternator charging failures
  • Starter motor problems
  • Vehicle not starting
  • Electrical short circuits
  • Battery drain issues
  • Blown fuses and relay faults
  • Dashboard warning lights
  • Wiring and connection damage
  • Intermittent electrical faults
