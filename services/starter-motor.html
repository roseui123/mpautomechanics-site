Professional Starter Motor Services

A faulty starter motor can leave your vehicle unable to start when you need it most. If your engine clicks, cranks slowly or fails to start completely, your starter motor or electrical starting system may require professional inspection.


MP Auto Mechanics provides expert starter motor diagnostics and repair services across Melbourne for all major vehicle makes and models.

Common Starter Motor Problems

  • Vehicle not starting
  • Clicking sound when turning the key
  • Slow engine cranking
  • Intermittent starting issues
  • Ignition switch problems
  • Loose or damaged starter wiring
  • Starter motor overheating
  • Battery and starter electrical faults

Accurate Diagnostics and Repairs

Starting problems are not always caused by the starter motor itself. Battery issues, wiring faults, ignition components and alternator problems can also affect starting performance.


We carefully inspect the full starting and charging system to accurately identify the fault before replacing components unnecessarily.
