Dashcam & Spoiler Installation – Melbourne Case Study

A customer visited MP Auto Mechanics seeking both a professional dashcam installation and a rear spoiler upgrade. The goal was to improve vehicle safety through continuous video recording while also enhancing the vehicle's appearance with a quality spoiler installation.


Combining multiple accessory installations during one visit allows all work to be completed efficiently while maintaining a clean factory-style finish.


Customer Requirements

The customer requested:

  • Professional dashcam installation.
  • Hidden wiring throughout the cabin.
  • Reliable power supply.
  • Secure spoiler installation.
  • Factory-style appearance.
  • No damage to vehicle trim or body panels.

Both installations required careful planning to achieve a professional finish.

Pre-Installation Inspection

The vehicle was inspected before beginning work to determine suitable mounting locations and cable routing paths.

Interior trim panels, airbag locations, fuse box access points and rear body mounting surfaces were evaluated to ensure a safe installation process.

This preparation helps avoid installation issues and ensures long-term reliability.

Installation Planning Process

Several factors were reviewed before installation:

  • Dashcam viewing position.
  • Fuse box power source selection.
  • Parking mode requirements.
  • Hidden cable routing paths.
  • Spoiler alignment points.
  • Mounting surface preparation.
  • Weather resistance considerations.

Careful planning ensures both functionality and a professional appearance.

Installation Procedure

The dashcam was mounted in the optimal position to provide maximum visibility while remaining discreet from the driver's view.

Power wiring was routed behind trim panels and connected using professional installation methods to maintain a clean interior appearance.

The spoiler installation required careful alignment and surface preparation before final mounting. Positioning was verified to ensure a symmetrical factory-style fit.

All components were checked and tested after installation to confirm proper operation and secure mounting.

Results After Installation

  • Professional dashcam operation.
  • Hidden wiring throughout the cabin.
  • Improved vehicle security.
  • Enhanced vehicle appearance.
  • Factory-style spoiler fitment.
  • Clean and reliable installation.

The customer was pleased with both the safety and visual improvements achieved through the installation.

Benefits of Dashcam and Spoiler Upgrades

Dashcams provide valuable video evidence in the event of accidents, parking incidents and unexpected road events.

A professionally installed spoiler can enhance vehicle appearance while complementing the overall styling of the car.

When installed correctly, both upgrades can add value, functionality and enjoyment to vehicle ownership.

Why Professional Installation Matters

Accessory installations can create problems when not performed correctly.

  • Visible wiring.
  • Incorrect camera positioning.
  • Electrical reliability issues.
  • Loose spoiler fitment.
  • Damage to trim panels.
  • Poor visual alignment.

Professional installation helps ensure a safe, reliable and attractive final result.
