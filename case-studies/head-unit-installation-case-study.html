Head Unit Installation and Infotainment Upgrade – Melbourne Case Study

This project involved upgrading a customer's factory multimedia system with a modern touchscreen head unit. The customer wanted improved connectivity, better audio quality and access to modern smartphone integration features.


Many factory-installed head units lack modern features such as Apple CarPlay, Android Auto and advanced Bluetooth functionality. A professionally installed head unit can significantly improve convenience and the overall driving experience.

Customer Request

The customer contacted MP Auto Mechanics seeking a multimedia system upgrade that would provide:

  • Apple CarPlay functionality.
  • Android Auto compatibility.
  • Bluetooth music streaming.
  • Hands-free phone calling.
  • Improved audio quality.
  • Modern touchscreen controls.

The customer also wanted the installation to maintain a factory-style appearance while preserving existing vehicle functions.

Vehicle Assessment

Before installation, the vehicle's existing head unit, dashboard layout and electrical connections were carefully inspected.

Compatibility checks were completed to ensure the new system would integrate correctly with the vehicle's electrical system and audio components.

All required installation accessories, mounting hardware and wiring interfaces were verified before work commenced.

Installation Procedure

The factory head unit was removed using professional disassembly procedures to prevent damage to dashboard components.

  • Factory head unit removed.
  • Dashboard trim carefully dismantled.
  • Vehicle wiring inspected.
  • Power and ground circuits verified.
  • Audio wiring connected.
  • Mounting hardware installed.
  • New head unit fitted securely.

All wiring connections were carefully secured to ensure long-term reliability and optimal performance.

System Testing and Configuration

Once installation was completed, the system underwent extensive testing to confirm proper operation.

  • Touchscreen responsiveness tested.
  • Bluetooth pairing verified.
  • Audio output tested.
  • USB connections checked.
  • Smartphone integration configured.
  • Vehicle electrical systems verified.

All features operated correctly and no electrical faults were present after installation.

Results After Installation

The upgraded multimedia system delivered significant improvements compared with the factory unit.

  • Modern touchscreen interface.
  • Enhanced audio performance.
  • Wireless connectivity features.
  • Improved navigation capabilities.
  • Hands-free communication.
  • Greater driver convenience.

The customer was extremely satisfied with the clean installation and additional functionality provided by the upgrade.

Benefits of a Professional Head Unit Installation

Modern infotainment upgrades provide numerous benefits for vehicle owners.

  • Improved smartphone integration.
  • Better navigation options.
  • Enhanced entertainment features.
  • Improved sound quality.
  • Safer hands-free operation.
  • Increased vehicle value.

Professional installation helps ensure all components operate correctly and prevents wiring issues that may occur with improper installations.

Why Proper Wiring Matters

Modern vehicles contain sophisticated electronic systems. Incorrect wiring can cause battery drain, audio issues, warning lights and other electrical problems.

At MP Auto Mechanics, all multimedia upgrades are installed using professional automotive electrical practices to ensure safe and reliable operation.
