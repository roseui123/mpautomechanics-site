Dashcam Installation – Melbourne Case Study

A customer contacted MP Auto Mechanics requesting a professional dashcam installation with hidden wiring and reliable power supply. The customer wanted a clean factory-style installation that would not interfere with vehicle safety systems or interior trim.


Modern dashcams provide valuable protection by recording driving events, parking incidents and unexpected accidents. Proper installation is important to ensure reliable operation and a professional appearance.


Customer Requirements

The customer requested:

  • Clean hidden wiring.
  • Professional mounting position.
  • Reliable power connection.
  • Parking mode capability.
  • No visible cables inside the cabin.
  • Factory-style appearance.

A complete installation plan was developed before beginning work.

Vehicle Inspection

Before installation, the vehicle interior was inspected to identify the safest cable routing path.

Special attention was given to airbag locations, trim panels, fuse box access and power supply options to ensure a safe and professional installation.

The chosen mounting position provided an unobstructed view while remaining discreet from the driver's seat.

Installation Planning Process

Several factors were evaluated before installation:

  • Windscreen mounting location.
  • Fuse box power source selection.
  • Parking mode requirements.
  • Cable routing pathways.
  • Airbag clearance verification.
  • Camera viewing angle adjustment.

The installation plan ensured both safety and reliability.

Installation Procedure

The dashcam was securely mounted in the optimal windscreen position to provide maximum road visibility.

Power wiring was carefully routed behind interior trim panels, keeping all cables hidden from view.

Where required, a hardwire kit was connected through the vehicle fuse box to provide reliable power and parking mode functionality.

After installation, camera settings were configured and tested to confirm proper recording operation.

Results After Installation

  • Professional factory-style appearance.
  • No visible wiring.
  • Reliable recording operation.
  • Improved vehicle security.
  • Parking mode functionality available.
  • Clean interior finish.

The customer was pleased with the final installation and the vehicle was returned ready for daily use.

Why Professional Dashcam Installation Matters

Improper dashcam installations can create several problems:

  • Visible loose cables.
  • Interference with airbags.
  • Poor camera positioning.
  • Unreliable power supply.
  • Battery drain issues.
  • Damage to interior trim.

Professional installation helps avoid these issues while providing a clean and reliable result.

Benefits of a Hardwired Dashcam

A professionally hardwired dashcam can provide continuous power, parking surveillance and a cleaner installation compared with plugging directly into a power socket.

Hardwired systems are commonly preferred by drivers seeking long-term reliability and enhanced vehicle security.
