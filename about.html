Who We Are

MP Auto Mechanics is a Melbourne-based mobile auto electrical service focused on diagnosing and repairing vehicle electrical problems. We assist vehicle owners with fault finding, battery issues, charging system repairs, wiring faults and accessory installations.

Our goal is to provide reliable workmanship and accurate diagnostics so customers can get back on the road safely and confidently. Every vehicle is inspected carefully to identify the true cause of the problem rather than simply replacing parts unnecessarily.

MP Auto Mechanics provides reliable and professional mobile auto electrical services for customers across Melbourne.


We work on vehicle electrical diagnostics, battery replacement, alternator repair, starter motor problems, lighting systems and wiring repairs.

Our Diagnostic Approach

Vehicle electrical faults can often be difficult to identify. Many symptoms may appear similar even though the underlying causes are completely different.

We use professional diagnostic procedures and testing equipment to inspect batteries, alternators, starter motors, electrical circuits and control systems. This helps reduce unnecessary repairs and improves repair accuracy.

Accurate diagnosis saves customers time and money while helping prevent recurring vehicle problems.

Vehicles We Work On

Our experience includes working with many vehicle manufacturers and various electrical systems found in modern vehicles.

Whether the issue involves charging systems, starting problems, wiring faults or accessory installations, we aim to provide practical and reliable solutions.
